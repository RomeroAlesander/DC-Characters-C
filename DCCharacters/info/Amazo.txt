Amazo
Abominable Android
Amazo is an evil android created by the mad scientist Professor Ivo to aid in his quest for inmortality. Amazo was originally programmer to absorb the abilities of the Justice League of America, whose energies Ivo hoped would bring him eternal life. However, Amazo can absorb the energies of any super-being.
